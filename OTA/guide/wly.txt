***************************************
How to install rising on wly:
***************************************

1- Unlock your bootloader
2- enter bootloader
3- fastboot flash recovery [recovery.img]
4- fastboot flash boot [boot.img]
5- fastboot flash vendor_boot [vendor_boot.img]
6- fastboot flash dtbo [dtbo.img]
7- fastboot reboot recovery
8- factory reset
9- apply update and sideload the rom -> adb sideload [rom.zip]
10- hit yes if you wanna flash gapps, etc
11- hit no to reboot and enjoy
