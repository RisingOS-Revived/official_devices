# RisingOS Installation Instructions for topaz/tapas

**Author:** @skwel24 x @mirabelle19999

---
 
## **Installation Methods**

**Flashing Steps:**
• Backup your Important files
• Boot to Custom Recovery
• Format Data/Wipe Internal, Cache, Dalvik, Metadata, etc.
• Flash the **REQUIRED** Firmware **(topaz 1.0.8.0 / tapas 1.0.10)**
• Flash **RisingOS Revived** ROM
• Reflash Custom Recovery
• Reboot Recovery
• Flash RO2RW
• Reboot Recovery
• Flash GApps of your choice (nikGapps Recommended)
• Format Data/Wipe Cache & Dalvik
• Reboot System

**Dirty Flash Steps: **
• Boot to Custom Recovery
• Flash **RisingOS Revived** ROM
• Reflash Custom Recovery
• Reboot to Recovery
• Flash RO2RW
• Reboot Recovery
• Flash GApps of your choice
• Reboot System
